antiX Help Videos



A sample of antiX help videos suitable for playing with Streamlight.

Welcome to antiX. I hope that this list of videos helps you in some way to become familiar with working with antiX. AntiX is not Windows, and it's not Ubuntu, and it's not a lot of other things. AntiX is a fantastically flexible system that will allow those of you with older or limited hardware to get the most out of your machine, and for those with more capable machines, it can allow you to do even more.


The videos at the top of the list, labeled antiX-15, are particular to that version of antiX. Those below, either not labeled specifically with a version number or labeled in the antiX 13.x series, contain information that is largely still relevant to the current release.


Each of these videos can be played using a lightweight video stream player called Streamlight. From the main antiX menu, simply select Streamlight from the Sound & Video menu. You will be asked how the app should run, either in Icon Mode or Run Once Mode. I suggest running in Icon mode the first time. This will put a Player icon in the system tray. Highlight a http address in this list and then left click the Streamlight tray icon. You will be asked whether to play or download the file, and then what size to view. Hint: the smaller size of the video, the easier to stream and play. The video should either download or begin playing shortly (depending on what option you chose).


And as always, if you have any questions, please post in the forums at antix.freeforums.org.


Thanks for Watching!
